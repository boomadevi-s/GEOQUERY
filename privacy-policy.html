Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use GeoQuery.

We collect personal information such as your location and search queries in order to provide relevant results for your queries. We may also collect your email address if you sign up for our service.

Data Usage

We use your data to improve the service and personalize your experience. We may also use your information to send you updates about new features or promotions.

Data Protection

We implement various security measures to ensure that your personal information is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies

GeoQuery uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the platform.
